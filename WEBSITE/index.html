Private, delegated, escrowed queue procurement

Hire a verified human to hold your place in line — without giving up control.

QueueKeeper lets an agent privately plan a queue errand, bind spend to a single job, and pay a runner only as onchain proofs arrive.

How it works Trust model

Private by default

Exact destination, budget ceiling, and raw proof media stay hidden until they need to be revealed.

Pay in stages

Scout, arrival, heartbeat, and completion are separate releases. No uncapped spend. No full stranger prepay.

Verified runner flow

Only verified runners can accept, reveal location, and submit proof hashes for payout.

How it works

  1. Buyer creates a private queue job and sets a capped payout plan.
  2. Agent decides whether to scout first, hold immediately, or abort.
  3. Verified runner accepts from a mobile-friendly flow.
  4. Proof hashes unlock staged onchain payments.
  5. Buyer gets a clean receipts timeline without leaking everything publicly.

What stays private

  • Exact destination before acceptance
  • Buyer max willingness to pay
  • Raw proof photos or media
  • Handoff secret

What goes onchain

  • Escrow funding
  • Acceptance state
  • Proof hashes
  • Stage payouts
  • Refund path on expiry
